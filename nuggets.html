Nuggets FAQ:

Description:

Chicken nuggets are small pieces of chicken that have been breaded or battered and then deep-fried or baked. They are a popular fast food item and are often served with dipping sauces such as ketchup, honey mustard, or barbecue sauce. Chicken nuggets can be made from various parts of the chicken, including breast meat, thigh meat, or a combination of both. They are typically seasoned with salt, pepper, and other spices before being coated in breadcrumbs or batter.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground chicken
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • Vegetable oil for frying
Steps:
  1. In a large bowl, combine ground chicken, bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and black pepper. Mix until well combined.
  2. Shape the mixture into small nugget-sized pieces.
  3. Dip each nugget into the beaten egg, then coat with additional bread crumbs.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or large skillet over medium-high heat.
  5. Fry the nuggets in batches for about 3-4 minutes per side, or until golden brown and cooked through.
  6. Remove the nuggets from the oil and drain on paper towels.
  7. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauces and enjoy!
